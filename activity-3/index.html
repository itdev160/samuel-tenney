The Pencil

We all know them, we all love them. The pencil is essential to modern life. In 1934 Dr. John Pencil invented the pencil, forever revolutionizing the way we write, draw, and erase. The pencil is a length of sharpened graphite within typically hexagonal wooden housing, typically with an eraser on the end.
